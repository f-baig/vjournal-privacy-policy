# Privacy Policy

**Voice Journal**
**Effective Date: March 5, 2026**

---

## Introduction

Voice Journal ("the App") is a voice-based journaling application for iOS developed by Farhan Baig. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

---

## Information We Collect

### Voice Recordings & Transcriptions

The App records audio and transcribes it into text using **Apple's on-device Speech Recognition**. Audio processing happens entirely on your device — no audio data is sent to external servers.

All voice recordings and their text transcriptions are stored **locally on your device** only.

### User Profile

The App stores a display name (default: "Me") that you may customize. This is stored locally on your device.

### Theme & Preferences

Your selected theme color and tutorial completion status are saved locally using standard iOS storage (UserDefaults).

### In-App Purchase Status

If you subscribe to premium features, your purchase status is managed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not collect or store payment information.

---

## How We Use Your Information

| Data | Purpose |
|------|---------|
| Voice recordings | Playback of your journal entries |
| Transcriptions | Display and search of journal entries |
| Display name | Personalization within the App |

---

## Third-Party Services

### Apple Speech Recognition

The App uses Apple's on-device speech recognition framework. Audio is processed locally and is **not** sent to Apple's servers. The App explicitly requires on-device recognition only.

### Apple StoreKit

In-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your payment details. See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).

---

## Data Storage & Security

- All journal data (audio files, transcriptions, metadata) is stored **locally on your device** using SwiftData and the iOS file system.
- The App does **not** use cloud storage, cloud backup, or remote databases.
- The App does **not** include any analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
- The App does **not** collect device identifiers or usage analytics.

---

## Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, share, or transfer your personal data to any third parties.

---

## Data Retention & Deletion

- All data is stored locally on your device and under your control.
- You can delete individual journal entries (including audio files) at any time from within the App.
- Uninstalling the App removes all associated data from your device.

---

## Permissions

The App requests the following device permissions:

| Permission | Reason |
|------------|--------|
| **Microphone** | Required to record voice journal entries |
| **Speech Recognition** | Required to transcribe recordings on-device |

Both permissions are required for core functionality and are used solely for the purposes described above.

---

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
